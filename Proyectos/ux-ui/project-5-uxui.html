Swap Now | App

Brief

Swap Now is an app designed to give clothing a second life by making it easier for users to exchange garments in a seamless and sustainable way. Through user research, wireframing, and prototyping, I developed an intuitive experience that encourages circular fashion while ensuring a smooth swapping process. The design focuses on usability, trust, and accessibility, making sustainable fashion more engaging and practical for everyone.

Timeline: 3 meses
Service: UX/UI - web designer
Role: Principal Designer
Tools: Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator.
