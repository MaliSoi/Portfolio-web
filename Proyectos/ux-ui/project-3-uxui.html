Epicurean Escape

Brief

Epicurean Escape is an e-commerce platform that helps users discover and shop for unique products from around the world, offering cultural richness without the need to travel. It’s more than a marketplace—it’s a window into global traditions through taste, craft, and design.

Timeline: 2 months
Service: UX/UI & Visual Designer
Role: Principal Designer
Tools: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, User Flows, Wireframing, Benchmarking

Inspirational moodboard

The Challenge

People are always searching for new, meaningful experiences—even when they don’t travel far.The challenge was to design a platform that would:

  • Inspire cultural curiosity.
  • Offer a simple, intuitive shopping experience.
  • Highlight authentic, global products in a way that feels personal, not generic.

How do you create an immersive yet minimal interface that weaves together storytelling, commerce, and cultural exploration—while also empowering small-scale creators around the world?

The Process

Moodboard & Visual Identityn
To shape the aesthetic tone, I developed a mood board—warm, earthy tones combined with organic textures, global iconography, and inviting typefaces. I also explored typography, icon sets, and image styles that conveyed authenticity.

Structure & Flows
I mapped the information architecture and crafted user flows to understand core journeys like discovering product stories, and checking out. Then I created low- to mid-fidelity wireframes to refine the layout and test usability.

Wireframes & Ideation
Finally, I brought the concept to life with high-fidelity designs that showcased real content, responsive interactions, and visual storytelling—balancing clarity and charm.

The Solution

The final product is a culturally rich e-commerce platform that allows users to browse by continent, discover artisanal products, and learn about the creators behind them. Key features include:

  • “Shop by Continent” navigation for exploration.
  • Embedded storytelling and curated collections.
  • Clean, intuitive UX with immersive visuals.

    • It addresses the original challenge by simplifying the shopping flow while embedding cultural storytelling and supporting global artisans.

Outcomes & Learnings

This project helped me explore an e-commerce model I find really interesting. It allowed me to combine structure, storytelling, and visual design into a focused desktop experience. Next time, I’d like to include usability testing to better evaluate and validate design decisions.
