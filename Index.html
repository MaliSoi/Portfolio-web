About Me

Hi! I'm Malala — a designer with over 15 years of experience in textile graphic design and a background in Fine Arts. I bring a strong aesthetic sensibility, a critical eye for detail, and a deep understanding of visual composition. Driven by curiosity and a desire to solve real-world problems, I’ve transitioned into UX/UI design and Front-End development—applying my creative vision to digital experiences that are both intuitive and visually engaging.

I’m currently working as a UX/UI & Front-End Intern at The Integrity Game, where I also contribute to graphic design. I'm continuously learning and building with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I’m especially passionate about projects in eCommerce and Retail—crafting user-centered, strategic solutions for digital catalogs and shopping experiences that connect people to products in meaningful ways.
